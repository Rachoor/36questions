What is 36 Questions?

What did you do with the last 45 minutes of your life? It's easy to let time slide by or spend it in ways that don't contribute to our overall happiness.

What if there was a way to spend 45 minutes and gain a life long connection, friend or even a lover?

Research has shown that intimacy a virtual stranger can be developed in a series of escalating personal questions.

Join us for a memorable evening where we'll match you with someone we think you'll like and you'll get to spend 45 minutes developing connection.

It may seem a little crazy, and definitely requires a risk for you to be vulnerable, but all good (great!) things require some risk, right?
